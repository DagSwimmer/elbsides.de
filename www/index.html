ELBSides

ELBSides is North Germany's computer security community conference. It will take place on September, 16th 2019 at the Side Hotel in Hamburg.

Follow us on Twitter for updates!

Sponsors

As a community event, we strive to keep admission as low as possible but the fixed costs are high. We are dependent on sponsors to make this event happen. More information will follow about sponsorshop opportunities in early 2019.
